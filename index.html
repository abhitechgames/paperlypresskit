Description

Paperly: Paper Plane Adventure will take you on a thrilling, physics-driven flight through a world made entirely of paper. Take off as a paper plane and master a unique flight mechanic as you glide through 11 ever-changing landscapes — each one more beautiful and challenging than the last.

Features

  • Fly a paper plane through stunning physics-driven gameplay
  • Explore 11 different landscapes — dry deserts, tropical forests, arctic ice caves and more
  • Collect Quantum as you fly and unlock new paper planes
  • 11 unlockable paper planes
  • 7 unique paper plane skins
  • Crash, learn, and head toward a better flight every run
  • Immersive wind sounds and real physics feedback
  • Two modes: Endless & Story
  • A deeply relaxing and meditative journey

Story

Paperly started as a passion project inspired by the simple joy of folding a piece of paper and watching it soar. The goal was to capture the exact feeling of being a paper plane — carried by the wind, learning from every crash, always taking flight again.

The team spent countless hours fine-tuning the physics to make every gust of wind, every dip, and every glide feel authentic. The background wind sound and physics together create a sensation that you are that paper airplane, navigating a vast, handcrafted world.

The vision has always been simple: cover all different landscapes, keep it pure, keep it fun — and make every player feel the freedom of flight.
